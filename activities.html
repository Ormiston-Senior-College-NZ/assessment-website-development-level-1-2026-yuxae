Activities & Festivals

The Club of Indian Diaspora organizes various cultural activities to celebrate and preserve Indian heritage. Our events include music performances, traditional dance shows, culinary experiences featuring authentic Indian food, and cultural presentations that educate and entertain our community.

Cultural Activities

  • Music, dance and food
  • Cultural presentations

Indian Festivals Celebrated

  • Holi is the festival of colors
  • Baisakhi is the Sikh New Year
  • Diwali is the festival of lights
  • Indian Diaspora Week celebrates the Indian diaspora
  • Indian Independence Day commemorates India's independence
